# Contributing to RADR

Thank you for improving the RADR open concept.

## License

By contributing documentation, data, code, or visuals to this repository, you
agree that your contribution is licensed under the
[MIT License](LICENSE) for public distribution.

If your employer or contract requires different terms, **do not contribute** until
you have written approval from FratresMedAI (see
[LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.md](LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.md) for prime collaboration).

## What we welcome

- Corrections to notional specs with traceability to annexes or JSON
- Link fixes, diagram clarity, and CONOPS wording
- Validation scripts and CI improvements
- References with citations (Annex E style)

## What to avoid

- Export-controlled or classified technical data
- Proprietary third-party data without permission
- Changes that present notional figures as tested or fielded performance

## Process

1. Fork and branch from `main`
2. Keep changes scoped to one topic per pull request
3. Ensure `baseline_systems.json` stays valid JSON if touched
4. Open a PR with a short summary and which annexes/docs you updated

## Prime or government contributors

If you are contributing on behalf of a defense prime under a PCA, coordinate with
your contracts lead and use the
[partnership inquiry template](https://github.com/FratresMedAI/RADR-mk.60/issues/new?template=partnership_inquiry.yml)
so contribution IP can be aligned with your agreement.
