## 2. Licensing model (two tiers)

### Tier A — Public (MIT)

Anyone may use, copy, modify, and distribute repository content under
[LICENSE](LICENSE), subject to the RADR program notice (notional study; no
procurement authority).

### Tier B — Prime collaboration (this document)

FratresMedAI offers **structured rights** beyond MIT for qualified primes that
execute a PCA. Typical grants (negotiated per deal):

| Phase | Purpose | Typical rights |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| **Evaluation** | Internal trade study, BD, capture | Non-exclusive, internal use of Background IP; no production |
| **Development** | Prototype, test, integration | Non-exclusive or field-limited exclusive **development license**; joint Foreground IP terms |
| **Production** | Manufacture for a named Program | **Sublicense** or **exclusive field-of-use** for defined configuration, geography, and term |

MIT alone does **not** grant trademark use, patent licenses, or production
authorization for weapon systems.

## 3. Background IP

- **Ownership:** FratresMedAI retains ownership of Background IP unless expressly
  assigned in writing.
- **Grant to Prime (under PCA):** A royalty-bearing or royalty-free license to
  use Background IP solely to perform the scoped Program work, including the
  right to create derivative works for that Program.
- **Reservation:** FratresMedAI may continue to publish, improve, and license
  Background IP to other primes on **non-conflicting** Programs unless an
  **exclusive field-of-use** is negotiated in writing.
- **Improvements to Background IP:** Improvements conceived solely by Prime
  personnel using Background IP are **licensed back** to FratresMedAI with a
  perpetual, worldwide license; FratresMedAI grants Prime a **sublicense** for
  the Program and agreed follow-on Programs.

---

## 4. Foreground IP

Default framework (customizable in PCA):

1. **Sole invention** by one party → inventing party owns; other party receives a
   **Program license** (and optional follow-on option).
2. **Joint invention** → **joint ownership** with each party free to practice
   internally; **external commercialization or government deliverables** require
   mutual written consent or pre-agreed revenue/role split.
3. **Government rights:** Flow-down of applicable FAR/DFARS or agency IP clauses
   as required by the funding instrument.

## 5. Patents and trademarks

- **Patents:** FratresMedAI may file on Background IP and joint Foreground IP.
  PCA includes a **patent license** (non-exclusive or exclusive by field) sufficient
  for Prime to perform and deliver the Program.
- **Trademarks:** **RADR** and associated branding remain FratresMedAI property.
  Prime may use names only as approved in writing for co-branded Program materials.
