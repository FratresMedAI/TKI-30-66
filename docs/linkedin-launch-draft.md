**RADR — Recoilless Anti-Drone Rocket (60 mm · Phase 0 concept)**

Squad and SOF need a **mid-range drone destroyer** between a machine gun and a Stinger — FPV, quads, loitering munitions, low-signature gliders. **RADR**: **lightweight, reusable** **recoilless** launcher + **guided flak rocket** — **speed-to-target**, **KISS**, **one-person reload**.

**What it is**
- **40 in** launcher · **18 in** round · **≤ 5.5 / 3.5 kg**  
- **100 mm IR F&F** · **moderate-maneuver** canards · fins **lock** on deploy  
- **Pyrotechnic dispersal** → **300 × 7 mm** alloy cubes, forward cone @ ~20 ft  
- **Radar or mm-wave proximity** + timed backup  
- Motor **2950–3050 N·s**, **~3.3 s** mildly progressive — **~330–350 m/s** @ 1000 m  
- **Gustav flip breech** · dual-trigger · **rocket retention stop**  

**What it looks like**  
Modernized **M1 Bazooka** — holo + fold-out viewer, pistol-style dual triggers, foregrip **+ / −**.
