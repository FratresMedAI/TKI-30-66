**RADR — Recoilless Anti-Drone Rocket (60 mm · Phase 0 concept)**

Squad and SOF need something between a machine gun and a Stinger for close-in drones — FPV, quads, loitering munitions, low-signature gliders. **RADR** is our answer: a **lightweight, reusable** shoulder-fired **recoilless** launcher firing a **guided flak rocket** optimized for **speed-to-target** and **KISS**, not high-agility dogfighting.

**What it is**
- **40 in** reusable launcher · **18 in** round · **≤ 5.5 / 3.5 kg** targets  
- **IR fire-and-forget** seeker · **radar or mm-wave proximity** fuze + timed backup  
- **300 × 7 mm** alloy cube forward cone at ~20 ft  
- **Progressive-burn** solid motor (Evolution Space propellant baseline)  
- **Gustav-style flip breech** · dual-trigger safety · **mechanical rocket retention stop** for slung carry  

**What it looks like**  
Modernized **M1 Bazooka** — **40 in** tube, fold-out **~4 in** viewfinder (camcorder-style), holo-fed display, **+ / −** zoom on foregrip.
