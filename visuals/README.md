## Forward device (visual direction — locked)

The hardware at the **muzzle** in side-profile art shall be interpreted as a **combined muzzle brake / blast deflector**:

- Retains **M1 Bazooka–like** forward fitting aesthetic  
- **Functional role:** manage recoilless launch venting, deflect forward blast away from support hand, reduce muzzle impulse on the tube  
- **Not** a purely cosmetic cap or unrelated blast shield  

Future renders (v12+) should label or shape this element clearly as a **vented brake/deflector** while keeping the approved Goodmk60 silhouette.
